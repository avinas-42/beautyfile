Beauty File — Privacy policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Data controller

Vinas Arnaud, acting as an individual (not a company). Contact: arnauz72@gmail.com

Summary

Beauty File is a Google Workspace add-on for Google Docs. It runs on Google Apps Script when you use the add-on in Docs. There is no separate “Beauty File” server that collects your documents: processing tied to the add-on happens within Google’s infrastructure when you use the product.

Data processed

  • The content of the Google Docs document that is open while you use the add-on, only as needed to apply the formatting you request in the sidebar.
  • Preferences stored by the script for your account (for example your last selected options), using Google Apps Script mechanisms (user properties).

Purposes

To apply formatting options (letter scope, font, size, bold, color) that you trigger in the add-on on the open document.

Retention

The data controller does not store your document contents on any server they operate. Documents and their history are retained by Google according to your account and the terms that apply to Google Workspace and Google Docs.

Sharing with third parties

Documents are not transferred to a third-party server operated by the data controller. The current version of the add-on does not use analytics or external APIs to process your documents.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights of access, rectification, or erasure regarding data processed through your Google account and documents you control. For any request related to the Beauty File add-on: arnauz72@gmail.com
